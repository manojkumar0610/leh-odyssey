D1
MAY 15
Bangalore → Pune
864 km · 16 hrs · Leave 4:30 AM → Arrive 11 PM
▼
The beast day. Leave at 4:30 AM — Bangalore traffic is non-existent at that hour. NH-48 is fast and smooth all the way. Pure endurance — flat, high-speed highway grinding. Fuel every 250 km (KTM 390 ADV range at highway speed in Ladakh conditions). Satara at ~380 km is the perfect lunch stop. Arrive Gulmohar Paradise by 11 PM. Straight to bed.
⚠ Leave 4:30 AM Sharp Longest Day — 864 km Fuel Stop: Satara ~380 km NH-48 All the Way

MAY 16
REST — Pune
Zero riding · Full recovery day
▼
Mandatory rest after 864 km. Sleep late. Eat well. Afternoon: inspect the bike thoroughly — chain tension, tyre pressure, brake pads, all fluid levels. Repack luggage — you'll know what you over-packed on Day 1. Pune has great food — Koregaon Park or FC Road for dinner.
Full Recovery Inspect Bike — Chain, Tyres, Brakes Repack Luggage

🌿 Scenic MP Route Nashik ~200 km — Fuel + Meal Indore Alternate (35 km extra)

✅ Jaipur Traffic Bypassed 🌅 Pushkar Evening Detour (15 km) Arrive 4 PM — Relaxed Evening

Arrive Amritsar by 6 PM. Freshen up. Head to Golden Temple for the night darshan — illuminated in gold, evening prayers. Stay near Golden Temple area.
✅ Delhi Completely Avoided Punjab Roads — Fast and Excellent Leave 5 AM — 12 hr Day 🌙 Golden Temple Night Visit

Morning in Srinagar — last walks, pack up. Leave 12 PM sharp. Cross Zoji La (3,528m) — aim to be through before 3 PM when army convoy traffic peaks. Good night's sleep before the Leh push tomorrow.

D9
MAY 23
Kargil → Leh
209 km · 4 hrs · Leave 7 AM → Arrive 12:30 PM ✓
▼
Leave Kargil at 7 AM. First stop: Mulbekh (~45 km) — 9-metre Maitreya Buddha carved into rock face, 7th century. Right on the road, 15-min stop, don't skip. Continue through Namika La and Fotu La (4,108m) — highest point on this highway. Then Lamayuru Monastery — moonscape landscape, one of Ladakh's oldest monasteries (15-min stop). Final stretch along Indus river valley into Leh. Arrive 12:30 PM.

Afternoon: Leh market → Leh Palace → Shanti Stupa at sunset (5:30–7 PM — stupa glows gold, get illuminated stupa after dark) → Indus viewpoint at dusk → dinner with friends.

Critical Knowledge
Tips & Prep
⚠ Read before you leave
📋
Permits
Get ALL ILPs at IMO Office Leh on May 24 — one visit: Nubra, Pangong, Hanle, Umling La, Tso Moriri. Carry 5 photocopies of each. Rohtang permit: himachalrohtang.nic.in — book for May 30 before leaving Leh.
🔧
KTM 390 ADV Specific
Spoke wheels = tube tyres — carry 2 spare tubes. Drop tyre pressure to 28–30 psi on gravel. Tank: 14.5L gives ~250–280 km in Ladakh (30% less than normal). WP suspension — run slightly softer on gravel. Run engine while using 12V pump.
🏥
Altitude & AMS
Consult doctor about Diamox before trip. Drink 4–5L water daily. No alcohol first 48 hrs in Leh. AMS symptoms: severe headache, vomiting, confusion — descend 500m immediately. Don't push through it. Friends start Diamox from May 22.
⛽
Fuel Strategy
5L metal jerry can mandatory. No official pump Leh to Tandi (~730 km via Tsomoriri route). Black market at Hanle, Korzok, Nyoma, Pang, Sarchu dhabas. Filter through cloth. Fuel is ₹15–20 costlier per litre in Ladakh.
📱
Connectivity
Only BSNL postpaid works beyond Leh. Jio/Airtel only in Leh city. Get BSNL SIM in Jammu or Leh. Download Maps.me offline before leaving Leh. Garmin inReach strongly recommended for Hanle–Umling–Tsomoriri belt.
🧥
Gear & Weather
May pass mornings: -5°C to 0°C. Thermal layers + windproof jacket essential. Rain gear for Zoji La and Rohtang. SPF 50+ mandatory — UV at 5,000m is extreme. Balaclava for Khardung La and Umling La.
🗺
Road Conditions
Verify Leh–Manali highway open status (BRO clears mid-May). Tsomoriri–Sumdo–Tso Kar: rough dirt 80 km. Shyok Valley: single lane, river crossings. Always start mountain stretches before 8 AM. Check BRO updates.
🏍
VRL Bike Courier
Call 1800 599 7800 before May 15 — book large cage slot for June 2, get reference number. Drain fuel completely. Take transit insurance. Cage system: bike rolls in, nobody touches it again until Bangalore. Arrives BLR June 7–9.
📸
Camera at Altitude
Insta360 + camera batteries lose 40–50% capacity at -5°C. Keep spare batteries in inner jacket pocket (body warmth) not in bag. Memory cards always in jacket pocket — never in checked luggage.
✈
Flight Back (Jun 3)
Wear riding gear on flight — allowed. Helmet as cabin bag (stuff gloves inside — within 7 kg limit). Camera always cabin — never check it. Viaterra tail bag as checked luggage (~12–14 kg, within free allowance).
